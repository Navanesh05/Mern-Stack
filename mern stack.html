About Anna College

Anna College is a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering the academic and personal growth of students. Established in 2000, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields of study. Our faculty comprises experienced educators who are committed to delivering world-class education and mentoring students towards success.

Courses Offered

Anna College offers a diverse range of programs to cater to the needs of our students. Below are some of the courses we offer:

Undergraduate Courses:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Arts in English Literature
  • Bachelor of Commerce
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Postgraduate Courses:

  1. Master of Science in Computer Science
  2. Master of Arts in History
  3. Master of Business Administration (MBA)
